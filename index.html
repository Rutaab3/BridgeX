Engineering Breakthroughs

Innovations that transformed bridge construction

100 BCE

Roman Arch Bridges

The Romans perfected the arch bridge, using concrete and stone to create structures that could span greater distances than ever before.

1779

Iron Bridge

The world's first iron bridge built in England marked the beginning of metal bridge construction during the Industrial Revolution.

1883

Brooklyn Bridge

This hybrid cable-stayed/suspension bridge introduced steel wire cables, revolutionizing long-span bridge construction.

2024

Smart Bridges

Modern bridges incorporate sensors and AI to monitor structural health in real-time, predicting maintenance needs before issues arise.
